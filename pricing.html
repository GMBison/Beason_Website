Plans and pricing

Choose the duration that fits your timeline and budget.

Beason pricing is simple: all paid plans unlock the full app. Students do not get a reduced version because they chose a shorter duration. The only difference is how long access lasts.

Android mobile app beta

The BEASON CBT Android mobile app is completely free for now.

The Android mobile build is still in public beta, so students can try it without paying while feedback helps improve the phone experience. Paid plans still apply to the full Windows desktop app.

Entry option

There is also a 1-day key for testing the experience first.

Students can try the platform before moving into a longer plan, which makes Beason friendlier for different budgets.

Ask about activation

Is the 1-month plan limited?

No. If you buy the 1-month key, you still get full access to the app during that month.

Why are the keys long?

The licensing system is sophisticated, so the activation keys are characteristically longer.

Does everything work offline?

Almost everything does. The main exception is the online Gemini tutor, which needs internet.
